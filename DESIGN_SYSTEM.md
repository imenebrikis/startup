# DarBelDar Design System

## Colors

- **Background (Main)**: #0A3D3D (Dark Teal)
- **Card Background**: #F7F7EC (Cream)
- **Accent/Secondary**: #ADEBB3 (Light Green)
- **Primary CTA**: #4B3FD8 (Purple)
- **Text (Main)**: #1a1a1a
- **Text (Muted)**: #717182
- **Borders**: #e5e7eb
- **Error/Alert**: #FEE2E2 bg, #EF4444 border, #991B1B text

## Typography

- **Heading Font**: 'Bricolage Grotesque', sans-serif (Weights: 700)
- **Body Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Base size: 14px)

## Components

- **Border Radius**:
  - Inputs/Search: 12px
  - Filter Pills & Buttons: 100px (fully rounded)
  - Property Cards: 24px
- **Buttons**: Full width (where applicable), padding 14px, font-size 15px, weight 600.
